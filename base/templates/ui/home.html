Kyabje Dungse Garab Rinpochhe, one of the original lineage holders of the Dudjom New Treasure lineage, formed a Krodhikali practitioner group for lay people in Thimphu, Bhutan to preserve Buddhism, especially the Dudjom New Treasure lineage. The group grew rapidly, and there are now over 20,000 Krodhikali practitioners in Bhutan, organized into 42 Dudjom Dharma Houses registered under Druk Throema Tshogpa.
