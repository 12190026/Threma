Info


Krodhikali Practitioner groups for lay people in Bhutan and in other parts of the world were founded by His Holiness Kyabje Dungse Garab Rinpochhe, one of the original lineage holders of the Dudjom New Treasure lineage. Years ago, when Kyabje Dungse Garab Rinpochhe was leading the Krodhikali Feast Offering (Tshog) with the monks, nuns, and yogis at Thimphu Memorial Chhoeten, Rinpochhe noticed the wives of the yogis taking part in the offering with full devotion.

At that moment, Rinpoche started to think about forming a group for lay people in order to preserve Buddhism in Bhutan, especially the Dudjom New Treasure lineage under the Nyingma (Vajrayana) tradition.
